About CorePRP

CorePRP is a dynamic public relations consultancy launched in 2025, specifically targeting South African startups, entrepreneurs, and small businesses in need of clear, strategic brand communication.


Based in Durban but offering services nationwide through digital platforms, our aim is to help emerging businesses stand out, connect with their audiences, and build powerful reputations in an increasingly competitive marketplace.


We understand the unique challenges faced by South African businesses and offer tailored PR solutions that drive visibility, engagement, and growth in our local context.
